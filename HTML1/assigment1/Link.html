About Education Background



APJ Abdul Kalam - Educational Background Kalam earned average grades, according to his teachers, but he was a hardworking, bright student who was also a good learner. Schwartz Higher Secondary School was Abdul Kalam's alma mater. Later, he graduated from Saint Joseph's College with a bachelor's degree in physics. He was said to be a bright and hardworking student who had a strong desire to learn and spend hours on his studies, especially mathematics. Kalam is popularly known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.In 2002, he was elected as India's 11th President. He was located at Rameswaram, in Tamil Nadu.
